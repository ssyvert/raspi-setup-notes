Downloaded Raspian from here:
https://www.raspberrypi.org/downloads/raspbian/

setup SD card with raspian jessie (terminal commands in mac)
unmount any disks that relate to the SD card
	> diskutil list
	> diskutil unmount /dev/disk2s1 
	> diskutil unmount /dev/disk2s2
Burn raspian image (rdisk2 may change depending on what's connected at the moment)
	> sudo dd bs=1m if=~/Downloads/2016-11-25-raspbian-jessie.img of=/dev/rdisk2
enable ssh
	> sudo touch /Volumes/Untitled/
	> sudo touch /Volumes/Untitled/
add wifi creds (add indented bit after command)
	> sudo nano /etc/wpa_supplicant/wpa_supplicant.conf
	network={
			ssid="network_name"
			psk="password"
	}

(put SD card in and boot raspi)
